Day-by-Day Custom Itinerary

Upon landing at Jomo Kenyatta International Airport (NBO), our private guide will assist with your transfers to Wilson Airport for your short, scenic flight over the Rift Valley directly into the Masai Mara airstrip.

  • Airport meet-and-greet service
  • Scenic domestic fly-in to Mara airstrip
  • Evening sunset game drive with predator sightings
  • Overnight stay in curated boutique luxury tented camp

Spend a full day in the reserve seeking lions, leopards, cheetahs, and herds of the Great Migration (seasonal). Enjoy a premium picnic bush-lunch under the shade of an acacia tree.

  • Early morning game drive for active hunts
  • Gourmet picnic lunch in the heart of the reserve
  • Optional sunset hot-air balloon safari (pre-booking recommended)
  • Pure-veg/Jain hot dinner served at the camp

Visit an authentic, non-touristy Masai Boma village. Learn about their ancient pastoral customs, traditional jumping dances, and craftsmanship directly from the village elders.

  • Interactive Masai village boma visit
  • Mara River crocodiles & hippo viewing walks
  • Sunset tea and cocktails atop a scenic hill overlook
  • Campfire storytelling with tribal warriors

Transfer into Olare Motorogi or Naboisho private conservancy. Enjoy absolute exclusivity with limited rovers, night-time game drives (not allowed in public reserves), and thrilling walking safaris.

  • Exhilarating guided walking safari with armed rangers
  • Exclusionary night-time game drives seeking nocturnal hunters
  • Bespoke camp accommodation inside Olare Motorogi

Board a morning flight via Nairobi directly to Amboseli National Park. Behold massive elephant bulls grazing peacefully in front of the snow-capped, towering Peak of Mount Kilimanjaro.

  • Domestic flight connection directly to Amboseli airstrip
  • Incredible photographic drives with giant elephant tuskers
  • Iconic Mount Kilimanjaro sunset backdrop
